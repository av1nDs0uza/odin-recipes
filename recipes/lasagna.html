Easy Lasagna

An easy lasagna recipe that saves me lots of time

Indulge in the comforting layers of flavor with this simple and delicious lasagna recipe. Start by boiling 9 lasagna noodles until al dente. Meanwhile, in a skillet, brown 1 pound of ground beef or Italian sausage with chopped onion and minced garlic.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound (about 450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2 cans (15 ounces each) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic. Cook until the onion is softened and the meat is browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, sugar, basil, salt, and black pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. In a bowl, mix together ricotta cheese and beaten egg.
  6. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce. Place three lasagna noodles on top.
  7. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a third of the mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses on top.
  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  9. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh parsley
